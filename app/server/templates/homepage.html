What we measure

To understand how government services are performing we measure 4 metrics.

Cost per transaction

What it costs government to run a service.

User satisfaction

Satisfaction is calculated by asking people to rate a service once they've finished using it.

Completion rate

The percentage of people who successfully complete a government service once they have started it.

Digital take-up

The percentage of people using government services online compared to other channels (eg phone or post).
